Tru Shine Services limited

Nourishing your organization to achieve maximum efficiency.

Tru Shine Services Limited was incorporated in 2010 with a primary objective of providing solutions to the needs and problems in Uganda and the Great Lakes region. Currently, Tru Shine Services Limited is a company that focuses on supplying electrical materials and spares, water supplies and chemicals, electronic hardware and components, ICT hardware and software solutions for security and business management, civil works and supplies for residential and industrial needs, medical supplies and equipment for private and government institutions, office equipment, stationery, consumables, and packaging materials for businesses throughout Uganda and the Great Lakes region.
Tru Shine Services Limited has been dedicated to meeting the diverse needs of its clients by offering a wide range of products and services. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction sets us apart as a reliable and trusted partner in the region. With a strong emphasis on professionalism and efficiency, we strive to deliver innovative solutions that exceed expectations and contribute to the growth and success of our clients' businesses.
